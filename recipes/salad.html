Simple Salad

Description

A simple salad with garlic lemon juice dressing.

Other salad greens can be substituted for those listed.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup arugula
  • 1/2 cup oakleaf lettuce
  • 1/2 romaine lettuce
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry greens, then tear or chop into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Crush garlic and mix with oil, lemon juice, and salt.
  3. Toss greens and dressing in a bowl.
